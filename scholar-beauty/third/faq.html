Products

What types of hair do you offer?

We offer premium quality human hair wigs, extensions, and weavons in various textures including straight, wavy, and curly. All our products are ethically sourced and carefully processed to maintain the highest quality standards.

How long do your products last?

With proper care and maintenance, our human hair products can last 12-18 months. The longevity depends on how well you follow our care instructions and how frequently you wear the product.

Can I color or style the hair?

Yes, our human hair products can be colored and styled just like natural hair. However, we recommend having this done by a professional to maintain the hair's quality and prevent damage.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. For in-store purchases, we also accept cash payments. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the US. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for an additional fee. International shipping times vary by location.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Please note that customers are responsible for any import duties or taxes.

Is shipping free?

We offer free standard shipping on orders over $200 within the US. International shipping and express shipping options are available at additional costs.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order through your account on our website.

Returns

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused products in their original packaging. Custom orders and final sale items cannot be returned.

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return, log into your account and fill out the return form, or contact our customer service team. We'll provide a return shipping label and instructions.

How long do refunds take?

Once we receive your return, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method and bank.

Can I exchange my product?

Yes, we offer exchanges for unused products within 30 days of purchase. Contact our customer service team to arrange an exchange.

Appointments

How do I book an appointment?

You can book appointments online through our website, by calling our salon, or using our mobile app. We recommend booking at least a week in advance for the best availability.

What happens during a consultation?

During a consultation, our experts will discuss your hair goals, assess your needs, and recommend the best products and styles for you. Consultations typically last 30-45 minutes.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment without any charge. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations for clients who cannot visit our salon in person. These can be scheduled through our website or by calling us.

Still Have Questions?

Our customer service team is here to help. Contact us for personalized assistance.
